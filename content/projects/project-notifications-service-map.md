My research into notification services gave our business partners a better understanding of the extent and causes of issues in these systems that they needed to advocate for change across multiple VA departments.
{{< /intro >}}

### Background

I worked with stakeholders across different teams to create a service map of the complex web of people, systems, and touch points across appointment notifications. I identified multiple pain points that were causing users to receive notifications inconsistently - sometimes 10 notifications for an upcoming appointment, then no notification of a cancellation. 

{{< img src="/img/notifications-touchpoints.png" alt="Service map diagram showing notification flows across systems and touchpoints" >}}

This was one piece of a major overhaul to the various notification systems. This work continues to inform ongoing improvements to those systems.
